Check out my favorite and most recent project- NortheastWS
A web app that mines 25 top news stories from twitter, groups them by similarity using an ensemble of ML models and informs you about the context of the news by studying the subjectivity of the tweet that links the story (official handle) and polarity of all the replies to said tweet. - News paper like delivery of day's top stories. - Prevents doom scrolling twitter for news.
